The Gap We're Filling

Industrial electronic repair has long operated without sector representation, industry standards, or a unified voice. Businesses operating in this space — from mining equipment to telecom infrastructure — work in isolation, competing without a shared identity or collective standing.

NACIER was created to bring these providers together, strengthen recognition of the sector, and support the long-term capability of industrial repair in Australia.

Member Benefits

What membership
actually gives you.

Six things your business gets from NACIER membership that you can't get operating independently.

Accreditation Badge
A verified, nationally recognised credential — for quotes, invoices, websites and tender submissions. A visible signal that your business meets a professional standard. Useful anywhere you need to justify your rates or stand above an unaccredited competitor.
Peer Network & Referrals
As NACIER membership grows, so does the opportunity to connect with other accredited businesses across Australia. When you need a trusted operator in another state or a collaborator on a complex job, NACIER facilitates that introduction. You're no longer operating without a professional network behind you.
Industry Representation
For the first time, commercial and industrial repair has a national body speaking on its behalf. Your membership funds and validates that representation — in government discussions, procurement policy, and industry forums where repair has previously had no voice.
Resources & Standards
Every accredited member receives the NACIER Code of Practice, their membership certificate, and accreditation documentation. As the alliance grows, members will be first to access resources and standards developed for the sector — the professional infrastructure that has never existed for commercial and industrial repair in Australia.
Tender & Procurement Support
When tendering for commercial or government work, your NACIER accreditation gives you something most competitors don't have — a formal credential from a national industry body. We provide the accreditation statement. You attach it. It's the difference between bidding with nothing behind you and bidding with recognised industry standing.
Collective Standing
There's a category of opportunity — government engagement, enterprise contracts, multi-state tenders — that individual repair businesses can't access alone. NACIER membership is the entry point to that table, backed by the credibility of a national body.
